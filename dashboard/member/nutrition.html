Weight Loss Meal Plan (Beginner)
High-protein • low sugar • daily steps
Goal: Fat loss (small calorie deficit)
Breakfast: Eggs + fruit
Lunch: Rice (small) + dhal + chicken/fish + salad
Snack: Yogurt / nuts / fruit
Dinner: Vegetables + protein
Rules: No soft drinks • sweets only weekly
Water: 2–3L • Walk: 8k steps • Sleep: 7–8h
Muscle Gain Meal Plan (Beginner)
Slight surplus • strength training
Goal: Muscle gain (slight surplus)
Breakfast: Oats + milk + banana + nuts
Lunch: Rice + dhal + chicken/fish + veggies
Snack: Peanut butter toast / curd / nuts
Dinner: Protein + carbs + vegetables
Post-workout: protein + carbs within 60 mins
Water: 2–3L • Strength: 3–4 days/week
Maintenance Meal Plan (Busy Worker)
Balanced plate • simple habits
Goal: Maintain weight & energy
Rule: Balanced plate → 50% veggies • 25% protein • 25% carbs
Snack: Fruit / yogurt / nuts (avoid biscuits)
Water: 2L • Steps: 6k–8k • Sleep: 7h
